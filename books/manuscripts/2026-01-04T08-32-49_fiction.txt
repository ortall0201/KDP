They paused at the crest of a hill, looking back toward Eldenwood—the home of her past, a foundation that would forever be a part of her. And then ahead, where the horizon kissed the sky, lay Faerie and all its mysteries, waiting to be uncovered.

"To new beginnings," Caelum echoed, and in his eyes, she saw the reflection of stars and dreams and the promise of love that transcended worlds.

Together, they stepped into the future, bound not by curses or by fate, but by a choice as pure and as true as any alchemical gold. The path woven between their hearts was laid out before them, a glittering thread in the vast tapestry of infinity. As they journeyed onward, the forest held its breath, and the world whispered of new tales waiting to be told. Their story, a melody of hearts entwined, was just beginning.
